Fractal Trees

Three.js experiments · iterations 01 → 06 · 15 · 16

01 — Baseline
EZ-Tree single hero
A single procedural tree with OrbitControls. The starting point — gets the asset pipeline, lighting, and color management working.
02 — Infinite Forest
Streaming FPS world
First-person walk through an endless forest. Chunked streaming, instanced trees, shared wind, real grass.
03 — FPS Experiment
Free + auto explore
Adds a cinematic auto-explore mode. State-machine avoidance, GSAP-driven buoyancy, ultra-dense grass, mode picker.
04 — Post Processing
HDR cinematic pipeline
DoF, bloom, vignette, film grain, chromatic aberration. Spatial grass variation. World-space dust motes.
05 — Graphics Tiers
Auto-only · pick your quality
Low / Medium / High preset selector. Fixes the v04 tone-mapping bug, tier-aware shadows, grass density, leaf count, post FX. Targets devices from budget mobile to gaming desktop.
06 — Godrays
Volumetric sun through canopy
Adds three-good-godrays on top of the v05 base. Tier-driven raymarch quality, alpha-tested leaf shadows so light pierces through the leaves, warm cream rays tuned to the directional sun.
15 — PS1
PlayStation-1 vibe
A tasteful PS1 pass over the terrain build: resolution-tied vertex jitter, low-res nearest upscale, 15-bit colour with ordered dither, and the modern post stripped. Live PS1 tuning panel on desktop.
16 — PS1 · explorations
FF7-style menu + fake sun shafts
PS1 explorations on top of 15: a Final-Fantasy-VII-style translucent blue menu over a foggy treeline, a mode switch with a Begin button, and PS1-faked sun shafts (crossed additive billboards, no volumetric godrays). Tuned look.
